About

I’m a third-year Financial Engineering student at Columbia University interested in how complex systems are built, scaled, and experienced by real users.

Academically, my background sits at the intersection of mathematics, computer science, and decision-making. Through coursework and hands-on projects, I’ve worked on backend systems, data pipelines, and infrastructure that prioritize reliability, performance, and clarity.

Alongside technical work, I’m drawn to product thinking—understanding user needs, defining success metrics, and translating abstract problems into structured, actionable solutions. This perspective shapes how I approach both engineering and leadership roles.

Outside of engineering, I explore visual storytelling through photography, digital design, and content strategy. These creative pursuits influence how I think about communication, narrative, and user experience across everything I build.
